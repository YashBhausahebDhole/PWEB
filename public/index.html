Our Services

Real-time Obscene Monitoring

Our system continuously monitors your child's online activities for any obscene content. This includes scanning messages, posts, and other interactions for inappropriate language or images. Alerts are sent immediately if any obscene content is detected, allowing you to take swift action.

Harmful Word Blocking

Our system blocks any harmful words or phrases from being used by your child. This feature helps prevent exposure to bullying, harassment, and other negative interactions. You can customize the list of blocked words to suit your family's needs.

Email Real-Time Notifications

We send instant email notifications to parents if any suspicious activity is detected. This includes attempts to access restricted content, use of harmful language, or interactions with unknown contacts. Stay informed and take action to protect your child.

Desktop Monitoring

Monitor desktop activities in real-time to ensure a safe and productive environment. Our system tracks application usage, browsing history, and more to provide insights and alerts for any concerning behavior.

Video Detection

Our advanced video detection system analyzes video content for inappropriate or harmful material. This feature ensures that your child is not exposed to unsafe or unsuitable videos online.
